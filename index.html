目的
Aims

为了增进演讲和口才水平，顺便增强一些其他方面的能力，同时遇见更多的朋友。

To improve speech and eloquence, enhance some other abilities by the way, and meet more friends.

指南
Reference

以下是几种这个网站的常规用法：

  • 在招新的时候使用，用于让新同学了解社团的基础信息
  • 将公众号的推文同步为Markdown格式，并且上传到本网站
  • 在办活动的时候同步社交媒体平台信息（我们有可能建立一些面向社外同学的群聊）
  • 在常规活动里记录本年活动

Here are some routine ways to use this webpage:

  • Use it when recruiting the fresh in order to inform them of some basic information of this community.
  • Align the passage we published in the Wechat Platform in the form of Markdown and upload it to this webpage in github.
  • Align Social Media Platform Information when organizing activities because we might establish QQgroups facing other students.
  • Take down activities in the Routine Activities page.
